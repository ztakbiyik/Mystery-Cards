**GUESS WHO? GUESS ME!**

Play now: https://mysterycards.netlify.app/

Ever wanted to play Guess Who with all the people you know? Whether it's a game night, a gossip session, or just a quick cure to the awkward silence, Mystery Cards are here to help.

All you need is two people with two phones that know what the internet is. Decide how scandalous you want the game to be by selecting the number of cards, then upload away your favourite images.
